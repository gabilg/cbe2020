Registration

We anticipate a registration fee of US$ 420.00 for regular attendees and US$ 250.00 for students, based on a participation of 80% regular attendees and 20% students. This will include two coffee breaks and lunch on the four days of the symposium, and a cocktail during each poster session. In addition, we plan to have a symposium dinner, and we will offer a range of activities for the off-day on Wednesday.
